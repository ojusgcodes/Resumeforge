Install the Auto-Apply Extension

It fills job applications for you. Setup takes about 2 minutes — just follow the steps below.


⬇  Download
Free · Chrome & Microsoft Edge
💡 A one-click “Add to Chrome” version is on the way. The quick setup works today.
▶ Watch: how to install & download (24 sec)
1

Download & unzip the file

Click the big green Download button above. Then find the downloaded resumeforge-extension.zip, right-click it and choose “Extract All” (or double-click to unzip). Remember the folder it creates.

2

Open your browser's extensions page

In a new tab, type chrome://extensions and press Enter.
On Microsoft Edge, use edge://extensions instead.

3

Turn on “Developer mode”

Flip the Developer mode switch — it's in the top-right corner of that page.

4

Click “Load unpacked”

A new button appears. Click “Load unpacked”, then select the resumeforge-extension folder you unzipped in step 1.

5

Pin it & come back here logged in

Click the puzzle-piece icon in your toolbar and pin ResumeForge Auto-Apply. Then open ResumeForge while logged in — the extension connects to your account automatically. No codes to copy.

✅ That's it — you're ready!

Open any job posting, click ⚡ Fill this form, review the fields, and submit. Done.
