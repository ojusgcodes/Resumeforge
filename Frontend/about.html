About ResumeForge

Everything ResumeForge can do

ResumeForge is an AI-powered career platform that turns your GitHub and LinkedIn into recruiter-ready resumes — then helps you find roles, tailor a unique resume for each job, and auto-fill and apply to the jobs you choose with a one-click Chrome extension. Here's the full picture, in detail.

Why we exist

Every AI can write you a resume.
Only ResumeForge can prove it's true — and get you ready to defend it.

Most resume tools hand you impressive-sounding claims and wish you luck. Then an interviewer asks “so what did you actually build?” — and it falls apart. ResumeForge is different. It turns your real GitHub work into a resume where every single line is backed by proof you can see.

🔬

A resume that can't lie

Connect your GitHub. We build an Evidence Vault from your real projects — and every bullet links to the repo, README, or live demo that proves it. No invented metrics. No fluff. Just click “Why this bullet?” and see the receipts.

🎯

Know if you'll get in — before you apply

Paste any job. Get an honest Apply / Improve / Skip verdict with a map of exactly where you're strong and where you're short. Stop wasting applications on jobs you can't win.

🚀

From “not qualified” to qualified

Missing a skill? We don't just tell you — we hand you a 5-day project plan to prove it, then update your evidence when you're done.

🎤

Walk in ready to defend every line

ResumeForge generates the exact questions an interviewer would ask about your resume — then scores your answers so nothing catches you off guard.

⚡

Apply to 50 jobs before lunch

1 · Build Your Resume with AI

From your real profile to a polished, ATS-friendly resume in seconds.

🤖

AI Resume Generation

Enter your GitHub URL and upload your LinkedIn, optionally with a target company and role. The AI reads everything and writes a complete, recruiter-ready resume tailored to that role — summary, skills, experience, projects and education.

📄

LinkedIn Import (PDF & Screenshots)

Upload your LinkedIn profile as a PDF (recommended) or as screenshots. The system reads it with AI, and falls back to local text extraction if needed — with safeguards on file size and page count so uploads stay reliable.

🐙

GitHub Analysis

Your GitHub is scanned for your username, repositories, top languages and skills, and bio. Those real projects and technologies become concrete, evidence-backed points on your resume — the kind interviewers actually ask about.

2 · Edit & Customize

Refine your resume by chatting, or design it freely like Canva.

💬

AI Chat Editor

A side panel where you simply tell the AI what to change — "make the summary shorter," "add more about leadership," "remove the hobbies" — and it rewrites your resume instantly.

  • Conversational, unlimited edits
  • One-click Undo to step back
  • Closable with a click, Esc, or the floating button
🧩

Editor 1 — Blocks (ATS-friendly)

A drag-and-drop editor where every section is a movable, resizable, editable block. Change fonts, sizes, colors and alignment, add text or images, and keep text crisp and machine-readable for ATS systems.

🖼️

Editor 2 — Canvas (free design)

A true Canva-style canvas. Every element has move, scale and rotate handles — flip images, layer elements, add photos and text anywhere, and design your resume with complete creative freedom.

3 · Professional Templates

Pick a design, download a polished PDF.

🎨

Six Resume Templates

Choose from a gallery with live previews and download your resume as a clean, print-ready PDF in any design:

  • Slate Header — dark banner with initials
  • Photo Header — avatar + label columns
  • Green Accent — sidebar layout
  • Classic — centered, serif, traditional
  • Mauve Elegant — refined serif band
  • Professional — bordered two-column
📥

One-Click PDF Download

Every template renders to a high-quality PDF in your browser. Tailored resumes are saved with professional file names automatically — like FirstName_LastName_Role_Resume.pdf — so they're ready to upload to any application.

4 · Find Jobs & Apply Smarter

The full job-hunt toolkit, built in.

🎯

AI Role Matching

The AI reads your profile and tells you which roles and companies you can realistically apply to right now — plus stretch roles you're close to, exactly which skills you'd need to add to reach them, and company types that fit you.

🔎

Multi-Source Job Search

Search live openings from multiple legal sources at once — aggregator APIs (Adzuna, Google-for-Jobs) and company ATS boards (Greenhouse, Lever) — merged and de-duplicated, each tagged with its source.

  • One-click search links for LinkedIn, Indeed, Internshala, Naukri, Wellfound & Y Combinator
  • Tick the jobs you want and save them
🗂️

Tailor & Apply

For every job you select, the AI writes a unique resume tailored to that exact role — reordering relevant projects, matching keywords, and rewriting your headline. Review each one, download it, and finalize it before applying.

5 · Auto-Apply Chrome Extension

Pick the jobs — the extension fills every application for you. You just review and hit submit.

⚡

One-Click Auto-Fill

Install our free Chrome extension and applying becomes effortless. Open any job posting and the extension reads the whole application form and fills it instantly — name, email, phone, LinkedIn, GitHub, location and more — and even attaches the right resume PDF. What used to take 15–20 minutes per application now takes seconds.

🎯

The Right Resume, Every Time

When you click Apply & Track on a job, it joins your Auto-Apply Queue together with the resume the AI tailored for that exact role. So when you fill that posting's form, the extension attaches the resume written specifically for that job — not a generic one.

🧠

Smart Enough for Any Form

It works across job sites — Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, Workday and most company career pages. A built-in AI engine reads each form and figures out what every field needs, so even applications it has never seen before get filled correctly.

✅

You Stay in Control

The extension never submits on its own. Confidently filled fields are marked green; anything sensitive or unusual — work authorization, salary, custom questions — is flagged yellow for a quick check. You review, fix the yellow ones, and click submit yourself. Safe, accurate, and within every site's rules.

📋

Apply to Many, Fast

Line up 5, 10, or more jobs in your queue, then breeze through them: open each posting, click Fill, review, submit. Every application you finish is logged automatically to your My Applications tracker so you always know where you stand.

🔌

Connects in One Step

Add the extension to Chrome, then simply open ResumeForge while logged in — it connects to your account automatically. No URLs, passwords, or codes to copy. The job-search panel even tells you when it's connected and ready.

6 · Your Account & Experience

Personal, polished, and yours.

🔐

Accounts & Login

Sign up and log in securely. Passwords are encrypted (never stored as plain text), and your session is remembered so you stay logged in when you return.

🌗

Light & Dark Mode

Switch between an elegant light theme and a warm dark mode with one click. Your choice is remembered across visits, and applies to the whole site.

🖥️

Resume Samples & Smart Layout

Browse real sample resumes in a gallery with click-to-enlarge previews. After generating, your resume stays pinned beside all the tools in a two-column layout — no endless scrolling.
