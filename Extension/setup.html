⚡ ResumeForge Auto-Apply

Two quick steps. After that, applying is just a couple of clicks per job.

1

Add the extension to Chrome

Open chrome://extensions → turn on Developer mode (top-right) → click Load unpacked → pick this Extension folder.

Then click the puzzle-piece icon in Chrome's toolbar and pin ResumeForge Auto-Apply so it's always one click away.

2

Open ResumeForge and log in

That's the whole connection step. 🎉

The moment you're signed in to ResumeForge in a tab, the extension grabs your account automatically — no URLs, passwords, or codes to copy.

You'll see ✅ Connected as you when you open the extension popup.

✅ That's it — here's how applying works

On ResumeForge:

Pick jobs → let it write a tailored resume for each → click Apply & Track. Each job (with its own resume) joins your Auto-Apply Queue.

On the job page:

A small panel appears bottom-right. Click ⚡ Fill this form. Green fields are done; yellow ones (salary, work authorization, custom questions) need a quick look. Fix those, then click the site's own Submit.

You always click Submit yourself — the extension never sends an application for you.

Stuck? You can also connect manually inside the extension popup under “Set it up manually”.
